Experience the soul of India through its villages

Mawlynnong, Meghalaya

Why Visit: Asia’s cleanest village 🌿

Language: Khasi

Food: Jadoh (rice with meat), bamboo shoot dishes

Culture: Matrilineal society where women lead

Festival: Wangala – harvest dance & music

Weather: Misty mornings, monsoon magic 🌧️

Attraction: Living root bridges & sky view tower

Khonoma, Nagaland

Why Visit: India’s first green village 🌱

Language: Angami

Food: Smoked pork, axone chutney

Culture: Proud warrior traditions

Festival: Sekrenyi – cleansing rituals & dances

Weather: Pleasantly cool all year

Attraction: Nature trails & Angami heritage

Pragpur, Himachal Pradesh

Why Visit: India’s first heritage village 🏛️

Language: Hindi, Kangri

Food: Himachali Dham feast

Culture: Ancient mansions & cobblestone streets

Festival: Lohri & Holi with folk dances

Weather: Crisp winters & breezy summers

Attraction: Heritage walks & craft bazaars

Poovar, Kerala

Why Visit: Where river meets the sea 🌊

Language: Malayalam

Food: Appam with fish curry

Culture: Backwater traditions & Ayurveda

Festival: Onam with boat races

Weather: Tropical, breezy evenings

Attraction: Poovar backwaters, golden beach

Hodka, Gujarat

Why Visit: Experience Rann Utsav 🎉

Language: Kutchi, Gujarati

Food: Bajra roti, Kachchhi dabeli

Culture: Crafts, embroidery & mud huts

Festival: White desert carnival

Weather: Desert climate, starlit skies ✨

Attraction: White Rann of Kutch & folk music

Ziro, Arunachal Pradesh

Why Visit: UNESCO tentative heritage site 🌄

Language: Apatani

Food: Bamboo shoot pickle, rice beer

Culture: Apatani tribe with unique farming techniques

Festival: Ziro Festival of Music 🎶

Weather: Pleasant summers, chilly winters

Attraction: Pine-clad valleys & cultural immersion
